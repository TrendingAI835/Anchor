# Anchor – Block Gambling. Regain Control.

Anchor is a powerful iOS app designed to help users block gambling websites and apps, reduce impulsive behavior, and regain control over their digital habits.

## Features
- Block gambling websites and apps
- System-level protection using Apple Screen Time
- Tamper-resistant blocking
- Simple onboarding
- Designed for long-term discipline

Anchor does not provide medical advice. It is a self-control and habit-support tool.
Anchor contains in app purchases and subscriptions
